Some types of music

Rock is a broad genre of popular music that originated as "rock and roll" in the United States in the late 1940s and early 1950s

Pop music is eclectic, often borrowing elements from urban, dance, rock, Latin, country, and other styles.

Jazz is a music genre that originated in the African-American communities of New Orleans, Louisiana, in the late 19th and early 20th centuries

Classical music generally refers to the art music of the Western world, considered to be distinct from Western folk music or popular music traditions.
